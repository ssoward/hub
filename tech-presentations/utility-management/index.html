End to End

From utility bill to resident statement

Capture invoice→ Validate & allocate→ Sub-meter reads→ Generate resident bill→ Collect via ACH→ Reconcile & report

Integration and automation connect each step to property-management and ERP systems — reducing manual handling and implementation friction.

The workflow page frames this as an interactive diagram with integration/automation and implementation considerations. The key idea: a single utility invoice fans out into many resident bills, then folds back into one reconciliation.

Payments

ACH payment processing

Benefits

  • Low per-transaction cost vs. cards
  • Automated disbursement & collection
  • NACHA / CTX file generation
  • Automated SFTP upload
  • Real-time status tracking

Challenges & solutions

  • Returns & retries → status tracking
  • Reconciliation → unique-ID matching
  • Compliance → NACHA formatting
  • Scale → batch file automation
ACH is the payment rail of choice because it's cheap and batchable. The hard parts are returns handling and reconciliation, both solved with automation.

AI Pipeline

The invoice processing pipeline

Ingest→ Extract (OCR/AI)→ Validate→ Urgency scoring→ Payment & NetSuite→ Reconcile
  • API-driven AP/AR posting with unique-ID reconciliation
  • Intelligent urgency scoring and automated prioritization
  • Automated record updates across PM-system integrations
This is the AI pipeline page: the invoice moves through extraction, validation, urgency scoring, then payment/NetSuite posting and reconciliation. AI mainly adds extraction accuracy and prioritization.

AI Transformation

Where AI moves the needle

Fraud detection

High-accuracy anomaly detection on invoice and payment data.

Prioritization

Urgency scoring routes the right invoices first.

Extraction

AI/OCR pulls structured data from unstructured bills.

Reconciliation

Automated matching reduces manual back-office work.

The AI transformation narrative: detection, prioritization, extraction, reconciliation. The value is less about flashy automation and more about removing manual toil at scale.

Strategy

The Capturis acquisition

Absorbing an invoice-capture provider brings an integration portfolio that must be migrated onto the platform over a multi-month timeline.

Core integration types

  • Utility provider feeds
  • Property-management systems
  • Payment / ERP endpoints

Migration requirements

  • Phased timeline (months)
  • Mapping & revalidation per integration
  • Strategic benefits vs. migration risk
Capturis is framed as an acquisition: the prize is the integration portfolio, the cost is a phased migration with per-integration mapping and revalidation.
